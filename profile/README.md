## What We Deliver

### Self-Hosted AI Stack
Complete infrastructure on your server:
- **Supabase** (database, auth, storage, vector store for RAG)
- **n8n** for business process automation
- **Custom AI agents** with access to your company data
- **Automatic TLS** via Caddy — no manual certificate management

### RAG Systems (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)
Your company knowledge, accessible to AI:
- Vector database with pgvector (native PostgreSQL)
- Semantic search API for internal documents
- n8n workflows for automated knowledge processing
- Connect to internal docs, processes, manuals

### Process Automation with AI
- Analysis and automation of existing workflows
- AI-assisted communication (email, CRM, document processing)
- Custom dashboards and reporting

### Consulting & Implementation
- AI strategy tailored to your business
- GDPR-compliant implementation
- Cybersecurity consulting (IEC 62443 certification in progress)
